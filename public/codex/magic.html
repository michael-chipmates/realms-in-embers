Emberlight

Emberlight is the realm’s magic, counted like coin. It flows every season: +1 from your own hearth, +2 from every Ember Spire (+2 more on an ember-site), +1 from an untapped ember-site, +1 per company of Adepts, +2 per Magus at your court, and whatever old relics kindle.

Learning: the Rites

A Rite is a working learned slowly. Start one, then pledge Emberlight season by season until the full price is paid. Up to three Rites are on offer at any time, leaning toward your creed’s teachings. Standing circles you rule cut every Rite’s price by 15% each, two circles at most.

Battle-magic

Battle spells cast themselves: when your army fields casters and your reserves can pay, the strongest affordable working weaves into the fight. Both sides, same rule. The odds preview names the spell and its price before you commit. You never pay for magic you did not see coming.

Realm workings

These you cast yourself, from the Magic screen (m), most of them onto a province. A working on cooldown returns after the listed number of your seasons.
